__**Archive Syndicate**__
"**Colors:** Beige
**Emblem:** Golden quill

In the year 221, a priestly order established the syndicate as a means of preserving and disseminating knowledge; books, scrolls, codexes, and other such publications would be archived and made available to everybody, regardless of belief. The Causeway has been under their care and protection ever since.

Beyond this, they have grown to become one of the most prominent political institutions in Ishaphere; its members are lifelong and admired in society for setting high standards of moral conduct."
