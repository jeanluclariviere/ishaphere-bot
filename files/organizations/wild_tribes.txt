Wild Tribes
**Colors:** Yellow, green, red and black.
___
**Emblem:** Club and Spear
___
The Goblin tribes of Nehrak are a significant political power in the world, but one that is considerably split. With no single tribe in charge, they frequently quarrel amongst themselves while competing for resources.

Nothing comes close to unifying them like war. For hundreds of years, they have been at odds with the Lizardfolks to the south, even going so far as to construct a wall spanning hundreds of kilometers, nicknamed "the collar," to keep them out.
