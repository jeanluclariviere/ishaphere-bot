__**Learning,  preparing and casting spells**__
**Learnining and preparing**
Must have an INT, WIS or CHA (as specified by class) of at least 10 + the spellâ€™s level to be able to learn or cast a spell.

**Casting**
When casting a spell, make a constitution save DC 8 + spell level. 
  - On a success, the spell is cast successfully, continue as normal
  - Critical successes does not expend the slot and inflicts double effect.
  - Critical failures causes the spell to fizzle, does not expend the slot, and causes you to roll on the Volatile Magic Table.
