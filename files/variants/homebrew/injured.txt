__**Injured**__
Roll a DC 10 constitution saving throw anytime you are hit with 10 or more damage in a single attack or effect. On a fail, you become injured.

**Once you are injured:**
  - You cannot use Grit Your Teeth
  - No roll you make benefits from an Attribute Bonus. Proficiency only.
  - Your Dexterity no longer adds to your Armor Class

**You are no longer injured when:**
  - You receive 10 or more points of healing.
  - You take a long rest.
